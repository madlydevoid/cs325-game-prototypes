Heart Attack!

  • High Concept: Avoid the hearts, you have one life, click to restart.
  • Theme: The hearts are fighting back against smoking, you mush survive.
  • Mandated Variety: The spawns come from the side of the screen and their direction is fixed, but beyond that the spawn is random.
  • Player Experience Goals: A quick few second game, if text was working then it would be to get the highscore.
  • Gameplay: Arrow keys move the cigarette (you) around. Should you get hit by a heart you lose. Clicking restarts the game.
  • Prototype Goal: Collision physics and more phaser basics.
  • Features: Nothing really besides the theme.
  • Setting/Premise: In a world of organs, the brains are the ruling class and decision makers, while the hearts tend to be the workers. The brains created a synthetic life form called the cigarette. Cruel to the core they pushed around the brains and abused the hearts.
  • Story: The hearts have begun to fight back against the cruel cigarettes. You play as a cigarette who does not want to be cruel, and just wants to live peacefully. Cursed by your birth as a cigarette, the hearts will come at you till you die.
  • Target Audience: People passing by on flash game sites.
  • Play Time: 10 seconds, max around 40 seconds
  • Strategies: Skill and staying in the center to watch oncoming threats.
